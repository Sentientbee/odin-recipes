Masala Dosa

Description

Dosas are Indian pancakes often filled with spicy potatoes and served with fresh chutney or sambar. This recipe is quick, easy and vegan-friendly.

  • Preparation Time: 30 mins
  • Cook Time: 1 hr 10 mins
  • Total Time: 1hr 40 mins
  • Serving: 8 Dosas
  • Diet: Vegetarian

Nutrional Information: Each serving provides 234 kcal, 8g protein, 32g carbohydrates (of which 4g sugars), 7.5g fat (of which 2.5g saturates), 4g fibre and 0.6g salt.

Ingredients

For potato filling

  • 500g/1lb 2oz potatoes, peeled and cut into 15mm/½in pieces
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsp black mustard seeds
  • 2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds
  • 8–10 fresh curry leaves (optional)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • ½ lemon, juice only
  • large handful fresh coriander (leaves and stalks), chopped
  • salt
For Dosas
  • 150g/5½oz gram flour (besan or chickpea flour)
  • 75g/2¾oz plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • sunflower oil, for greasing
For the coriander and mint chutney
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tsp caster sugar
  • 150g/5½oz coconut yoghurt
  • ½ lime, juice only
  • freshly ground black pepper
